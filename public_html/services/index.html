02

Credit Bureau Listings

When a debtor refuses to engage or ignores repeated contact attempts, a credit bureau listing becomes one of the most effective tools available. We handle the listing process on your behalf, ensuring non-paying debtors are recorded on South Africa's credit bureaus in accordance with the National Credit Act.

The impact of a bureau listing is significant. It affects a debtor's credit score, limits their ability to access new credit, secure rental agreements, or obtain services that require creditworthiness checks. This creates a powerful and entirely legitimate incentive for the debtor to settle the outstanding balance — often prompting action where previous contact attempts had failed.

We manage the full process: notification to the debtor, submission to the bureau, and removal once the account is settled. You are kept informed throughout, and the outcome is documented in your monthly report.

  • Compliant with the National Credit Act
  • Full listing-to-removal process managed for you
  • Effective against persistent non-payers
  • Documented and reported monthly

Tracing Services

Some debtors don't just avoid paying — they disappear. They change phone numbers, move addresses, and make themselves impossible to contact through the information you have on file. Our skip-tracing service is designed to find them.

We use legitimate investigative methods and access to South Africa's available data infrastructure to locate debtors who have relocated, changed contact details, or otherwise made themselves unreachable. Our tracing capabilities extend nationwide — wherever the debtor has moved, we work to find them.

Once a debtor is traced, we resume the standard recovery process immediately. Tracing is integrated into our overall collection workflow, so there's no gap in the recovery effort and no delay in picking up where we left off.

Regular Follow-Ups

Debt recovery is rarely a one-contact exercise. The vast majority of successful collections happen because someone kept following up — consistently, professionally, and without giving the debtor the opportunity to forget or ignore the obligation. That's exactly what we do.

We build a structured contact schedule for every account: initial contact, reminder calls at defined intervals, written follow-ups via email and letter, and escalation triggers when responses stall. Every contact is documented, timestamped, and retained as a complete record of recovery efforts.

This documented trail also serves a practical legal purpose — it demonstrates that due process has been followed if further legal steps ever become necessary. More importantly, it simply works. Consistent pressure within a structured framework is the most reliable path to recovery.

  • Pre-defined contact schedules per account
  • Every contact logged with date, time, and outcome
  • Automatic escalation when accounts stall
  • Admissible audit trail for legal proceedings

Monthly Payments to Clients

Recovering your money is only half the job — getting it back to you quickly and reliably is equally important. Every rand we collect on your behalf is held in a separate account and transferred directly to you on a monthly basis.

Our payment process is straightforward. At the close of each month, we calculate the total collected from your accounts, deduct our agreed commission, and transfer the balance directly to your nominated bank account. The accompanying payment summary matches line-for-line with your monthly report, giving you a complete and auditable record of every transaction.

There are no delays, no holding back funds, and no administrative complexity on your side. You simply receive a transfer and a clear summary. Your money gets back to your business where it belongs.
