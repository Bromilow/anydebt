2. Our Services

Any Collections Pty Ltd provides the following debt recovery services to clients:

  • Debt Collection: Professional outreach to debtors using ethical and legally compliant methods to recover outstanding amounts owed to the Client.
  • Credit Bureau Listings: Submission of qualifying debtor information to registered South African credit bureaus, in accordance with applicable legislation, to encourage repayment.
  • Tracing Services: Locating debtors who have moved, changed contact details, or become uncontactable, using registered tracing agents and databases.
  • Regular Follow-Ups: Structured and consistent contact with debtors on behalf of the Client, ensuring no account goes dormant.
  • Monthly Reporting: Delivery of a detailed report to the Client each month, covering the status of all accounts and any funds recovered.
  • Monthly Payments: Recovered funds, less agreed commission, are paid directly to the Client on a monthly basis.

The scope of services for each Client will be agreed upon at the commencement of the engagement and documented in a service agreement.

3. Client Obligations

To enable the Company to perform its services effectively, the Client agrees to:

  • Provide Accurate Information: Supply complete, accurate, and up-to-date information about each debtor, including name, identity number, contact details, address, and the amount and nature of the outstanding debt.
  • Ownership of Debt: Confirm that all debts handed over for collection are legitimately owed to the Client and are free from any dispute that has not been disclosed.
  • Cooperation: Cooperate with the Company by responding promptly to requests for additional information, documentation, or instructions relating to specific accounts.
  • Direct Payment Notification: Inform the Company immediately if a debtor makes a payment directly to the Client, so that commission can be calculated and records updated accordingly.
  • Compliance with Law: Ensure that all information provided to the Company has been obtained lawfully and that its disclosure and use for debt collection purposes complies with POPIA and any other applicable legislation.
  • Non-Interference: Refrain from contacting debtors directly in respect of debts that have been handed over to the Company, unless expressly agreed in writing.

Our services operate on a commission-only basis. There are no upfront fees, retainers, or monthly charges unless otherwise expressly agreed in writing.

  • Commission Rate: The commission percentage applicable to each account or portfolio will be agreed upon between the Company and the Client prior to commencement of collection activities, and documented in a written service agreement.
  • No Recovery, No Fee: If we are unable to recover an outstanding amount, no commission is charged. The Client bears no financial risk for accounts that remain unrecovered.
  • Direct Payments: Where a debtor makes a payment directly to the Client (rather than through the Company), the Client is obligated to notify the Company and pay the agreed commission on that recovered amount within 14 days.
  • Disbursements: Any exceptional disbursements (such as court filing fees, should legal proceedings be required and agreed) will be discussed with and approved by the Client in advance before being incurred.
  • Payment to Client: Recovered funds, less agreed commission, will be paid to the Client's nominated bank account on or before the last business day of each calendar month.
